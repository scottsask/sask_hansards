





























































Hansard: May 16, 1991


 

 

May 16/91 
EVENING SITTING 

 
Mr. Lingenfelter: -- Mr. Speaker, I move this House do now adjourn. 
 
The division bells rang from 7:01 p.m. until 9:59 p.m. 
 
The Speaker: -- It being 10 o'clock and the automatic adjournment hour 
under rule 3(3) having been reached, the motion to adjourn lapses. 
 
The Assembly adjourned at 10 p.m.
