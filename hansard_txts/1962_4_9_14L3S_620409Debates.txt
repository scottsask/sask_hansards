




















































Hansard: April 09, 1962


LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF SASKATCHEWAN 

Third Session — Fourteenth Legislature 

32nd Day 

 

Monday, April 9, 1962. 

 

The Assembly met at 2:30 o’clock p.m. 

 

On the Orders of the Day: 

 

Hon. A.E. Blakeney (Provincial Treasurer): — Mr. Speaker, I would like to advise that I have a 

message from His Honour, the Lieutenant Governor. 

 

Mr. Speaker: — 

 

The Lieutenant Governor transmits further supplementary estimates of certain sums required for the 

service of the province for the twelve months ending March 31, 1962, and recommends the same to 

the Legislative Assembly. 

 

Hon. Mr. Blakeney: — Mr. Speaker, I move, seconded by the Hon. Mr. Lloyd that His Honour’s 

message and the further supplementary estimates be referred to the committee of supply. 

 

Motion agreed to. 

 

The Assembly adjourned at 10:00 o’clock p.m.
