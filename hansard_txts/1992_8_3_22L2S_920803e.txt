Bill No. 21 

 

The Assembly resumed the adjourned debate on the proposed 

motion by the Hon. Mr. Lingenfelter that Bill No. 21 -- An Act 

to continue SaskEnergy Incorporated, to make certain 

consequential amendments to certain Acts resulting from 

that continuance and to validate certain transactions 

involving SaskEnergy Incorporated be now read a second 

time. 

 

Mr. Britton: -- Thank you, Mr. Speaker.  Mr. Speaker, there's 

a lot to be talked about in this Bill and we are going to . . . 

There's a lot of things we don't really like in this Bill and we'll 

be talking about this for some time. 

 

Mr. Speaker, I'm not prepared to do it, so I adjourn debate. 

 

The Speaker: -- Order, order.  Order, order.  Order.
